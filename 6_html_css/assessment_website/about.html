Our Story

From Dream to Reality

Sunny Side Bistro began as a dream shared by childhood friends Maria Rodriguez and James Thompson in 2018. After years of working in restaurants across Austin, they envisioned a place where community could gather over exceptional food made with care and creativity.

What started as a small 30-seat restaurant in downtown Austin has grown into a beloved local institution with three locations. Despite our growth, we've never lost sight of our original mission: to serve fresh, locally-sourced food that brings people together.

Every dish we serve reflects our commitment to supporting local farmers, using sustainable practices, and creating memorable dining experiences for our guests.

Our Mission

Fresh & Local

We partner with local farms and suppliers within 50 miles of Austin to ensure the freshest ingredients on your plate. Our menu changes seasonally to highlight the best of what Texas has to offer.

Community First

We believe great food brings people together. Whether you're grabbing coffee with a friend, celebrating a special occasion, or enjoying a quiet meal alone, we want every guest to feel at home.

Sustainable Practices

From composting our food waste to using eco-friendly packaging, we're committed to reducing our environmental impact while serving our community.
